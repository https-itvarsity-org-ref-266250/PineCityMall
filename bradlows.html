WHO ARE WE ?

Bradlows has been connecting South Africans with their favourite furnishings for generations. That's why the vast majority of our customers are also our friends.Drop into Bradlows and chat with our knowledgeable staff, learn how we produce top quality furniture at affordable prices.Talk about value!

Choose from our wide range of furniture and appliances from the bedroom to the kitchen, dining room to the lounge, all the matching accessories: coffee tables, wall units and audio and visual components, tailored to your taste and your budget.There are luxurious leather, imported fabrics and contemporary or classic designs to choose from. It's all as a result of Bradlows unequalled experience in choosing stylish, quality furniture you can afford

With the history tha spans 120 years of bringing affordable nd quality furniture and appliances to the people of South Africa, Bradlows has carved itself into the heritage of our country. From humble beginnings in Johannesburg Central, during the gold rush in 1901, the supply of household goods and comforts have always been a neccesity and thanks to David Bradlows we will continue the tradition.

It's with the loyalty of everyday history.Looking back servers as a reminder, not only of how how far we have come but how far we still have to go.
