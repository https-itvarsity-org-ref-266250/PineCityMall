WHO ARE WE ?

McDonald's Corporation is an American multinationL fast food chain, founded in 1940 as a restaurant operated by richard and Maurice McDonald, in San Bernadino, California, United.They rechistrened their business as a hamburger stand, and later turned the company into a franchise, with the Golden Arches logo being introduced in 1953 at a location in Phoenix, Arizona.In 1953, Ray Kroc, a businessman, joined the company as a franchise agent and processed to purchase the chain from the McDonald brothers. McDonald's previous headquarters was based in Oak Brook, illnois, but the company moved its global headquarters to Chicago in June 2018.

McDonalds is also a real estate company through its ownership of around 70% of restaurant buildings and 45% of the underlying land(which it leases to its fanchisees).

McDonald's is the world's largest fast food restaurant chain, serving over 69million customers daily in over 100 countries in more than 40,000 outlets as of 2021.McDonald's is best known for its hamburgers, cheeseburgers and french fries, alrough their menu also includes other items like chicken, fish , fruit and salads.Their best-sellin licensed item are their french fries, folowed by the Big Mac.
