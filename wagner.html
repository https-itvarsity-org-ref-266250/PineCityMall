WHO ARE WE ?

Wagner Elecric Corporation was an electric equipment manufacturing firm established in 1891 tha became part of Studebakwer-Washington in 1967.

Today Wagner is part of the Federal-Mogul Motorparts group.Under the name Wagner Lighting Products, some of their brands include TruView, BriteLite, HalogenGold and LzerBlue. They also make brake pads under the name Wagner Brake Products.
