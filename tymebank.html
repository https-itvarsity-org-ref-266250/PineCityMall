WHO ARE WE ?

TYMEBANK is an exclusively digital retail bank based in South Africa.

TYMEBANK offers a transactional account, money transfer service, savings acoount and educational App. There are plans to introduce credit cards, unsecured loans, and loan to small and medium-sized enterprises(SME)

TYMEBANK is the first bank in South Africa to receive a full banking licence in almost 20 years and has been signing up approximately 100,000 customers a month since its launch in February 2019.
